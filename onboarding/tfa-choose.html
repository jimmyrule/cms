When you log in to an online account, you're proving that you are who you say you are. Traditionally that is achieved by a username and a password.

When the account you’re accessing includes sensitive information, it’s expected that you provide an extra piece of information. The EHC Hub does this by asking you to enter a unique code each time you log in.
