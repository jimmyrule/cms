Panel meetings

Panel meetings are usually convened to consider requests for an EHC needs assessment, an EHC Plan, a change of educational placement or level of support, a personal budget or changes that might be needed for an existing EHC Plan. Information can be shared with panel members before the meeting so that they can read through everything and prepare for the meeting.

This area allows you to manage future, current and previous panel meetings and interact with panel meetings for which you are a panel member.
